# PPT Skill Research

- 121 concrete PPT / Slides skills were retained after removing generic indexes.
- The ecosystem is large, but most entries are variations of 8-10 workflow types.

# Common Workflow

- Route the task before generating.
- Build a structured slide plan.
- Choose native PPTX, HTML, image-first, Google Slides, or reconstruction route.
- Render and QA before delivery.

# All-in-One Design

- Default to editable PPTX.
- Preserve `slide_plan.json`.
- Use rendered QA and targeted repair.
- Keep image-first as an optional mode.
