# Business Mode

Use for pitch decks, customer proposals, board updates, consulting decks, product strategy, and executive reporting.

## Common Structures

### Pitch Deck

1. Problem.
2. Insight.
3. Solution.
4. Product.
5. Market.
6. Traction.
7. Business model.
8. Competition.
9. Team.
10. Ask.

### Executive Update

1. Decision needed.
2. Current state.
3. Key metrics.
4. Risks and blockers.
5. Options.
6. Recommendation.
7. Next actions.

### Customer Proposal

1. Customer context.
2. Pain points.
3. Proposed solution.
4. Implementation plan.
5. Proof and case studies.
6. Timeline.
7. Commercial terms or next step.

## Rules

- Lead with the decision or business outcome.
- Use action titles.
- Keep metrics consistent across slides.
- Make risks explicit.
- Separate facts, assumptions, and recommendations.
